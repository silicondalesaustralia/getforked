# Agent Rules

## About / Origin Story Rule

Add a "Who are we?" section to the homepage and create a dedicated `/about/` page.

The positioning should explain that GetForked was started by developers and business owners who originally built their own SaaS replacements, automations, dashboards, connectors and internal tools to reduce costs and regain ownership of business workflows.

The tone should be practical, operator-led and trustworthy. Avoid sounding like a generic software agency or hype-driven AI startup.

Core message:
- We experienced the SaaS tax ourselves.
- We built our own replacements first.
- We refined the process.
- Now we help other business owners save time, reduce software waste and own the systems their businesses depend on.

Use "business owners and developers" as the founding frame.

Do not claim exact savings, number of clients, years in business, or proprietary process unless those facts are provided.

Use "own the workflow" and "reduce the SaaS tax" as repeated message anchors.
